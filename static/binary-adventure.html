Section 1

💡 What's a Bit?

A bit is the smallest piece of information a computer can store — it's either OFF (0) or ON (1). Think of it like a light switch. Click the bulbs below!

Click to toggle the lights

Each ON bulb = 1, each OFF bulb = 0

Key Facts

  • ⚡ 1 bit = one 0 or 1
  • 📦 8 bits = 1 byte (enough to store one letter)
  • 💾 1,000,000 bytes = 1 megabyte (one song!)
  • 🧠 Everything on your computer is made of bits: photos, videos, code, emoji
