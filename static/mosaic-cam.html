Mosaic Camera

An interactive webcam mosaic effect

How It Works

  • Your webcam feed plays normally
  • Click & hold (or press any key) to activate the mosaic
  • Drag left ↔ right to change mosaic block size
  • Drag up ↕ down to shift the color tone
  • Release to return to normal view
