Maze Escape

2-Player competitive maze game

How to Play

  • Move with W A S D or Arrow Keys
  • Navigate from S (start) to G (goal)
  • Walls # block movement — no steps wasted on walls
  • Fog of war — you can only see 3 cells around you!
  • Player 1 goes first, then Player 2 gets the same maze
  • Fastest time wins — steps as a tiebreaker!
